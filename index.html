We’re a small practice taking a personal approach to design and construction, guiding projects successfully through all the hurdles until the  completion of construction. We take our integrity seriously and deliver. Our services are broad, including the complete design of buildings and interiors, working with heritage, landscape as well as coordinating construction and contracts.

We focus on delivering engaging and highly efficient buildings, with an awareness of costs. We consider our best work to be of least-impact, efficient, budget driven and imbued with joy.

Michael is a registered Architect in Victoria of 13 years. He relishes working closely with clients and builders to take the stress out of the process.  He also is a Certified Passivhaus Designer, passionate about building detail and advancing sustainability. Phil joins him as a recently registered Architect.
